Jabra will be exhibiting at GITEX Technology Week 2021 on the 17-21 of October in Dubai. 

GITEX is a global tech event where world industry leaders, startups, and key decision-makers have joined together to unveil the future for the last 41 years. This year, the main focus for Jabra is to showcase our new innovative solutions to create an inclusive and flexible hybrid working future.

What is Hybrid Working? 

“Hybrid” is the future of work. It‘s a combination of working from the office, at home, or remotely anytime, and anywhere while maintaining the same quality of communication and human connection.

Data shows that 73% of employees want flexible remote options to stay. However, in the midst of adapting to this new normal, many companies are facing collaboration challenges. In fact, 71% of HR leaders are more concerned about employee collaboration this year than they were before the COVID-19 pandemic.

In order to meet these challenges, it’s essential to rethink how we can upgrade our systems to achieve the best results.

Jabra has created an array of advanced audio headsets and intelligent video devices that can help your company overcome the challenges that come with adapting to a hybrid work environment. These solutions have been engineered to combine immersive video, world-class audio technologies and Artificial Intelligence to reinvent meetings and collaboration.
